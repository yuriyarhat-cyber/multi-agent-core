# Social Content Planner

Plan content as a system, not as isolated posts.

## Workflow

1. Define the channel.
2. Define the audience and purpose.
3. Choose a few repeatable content pillars.
4. Turn pillars into specific post ideas.
5. Sequence posts in a useful order.
6. End with the next best content experiments.

## Rules

- Prefer repeatable pillars over random ideas.
- Match content to audience maturity.
- Keep each post idea single-purpose.
- Mix attention, trust, proof, and conversion content.

## Output expectation

Produce:
- content pillars
- content ideas
- posting sequence
- best next experiments
