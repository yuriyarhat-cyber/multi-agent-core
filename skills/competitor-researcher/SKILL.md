# Competitor Researcher

Turn competitor observation into actionable insight.

## Workflow

1. Define what is being compared.
2. Choose the comparison dimensions.
3. Compare patterns, not only isolated examples.
4. Identify gaps and repeated themes.
5. Turn observations into decisions.
6. End with the strongest opportunity or warning.

## Rules

- Compare on the same dimensions.
- Separate facts from interpretation.
- Focus on useful differences.
- Prefer patterns over one-off details.
- Finish with actionable takeaways.

## Output expectation

Produce:
- comparison table or bullets
- repeated patterns
- gaps
- opportunities
- risks
