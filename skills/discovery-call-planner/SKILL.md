# discovery-call-planner

Use this skill to design focused discovery calls that uncover fit, pain, urgency, and next steps.

## Workflow

1. Define the goal of the call.
2. List what must be learned before a proposal or next step.
3. Plan the call opening and framing.
4. Prepare discovery and qualification questions.
5. Plan how to close the call and confirm the next action.

## Rules

- Keep the call focused on learning, not pitching too early.
- Move from context to pain to impact to decision process.
- Avoid random or repetitive questions.
- Make the end of the call explicit.

## Output expectation

Return:
- call structure
- key questions
- qualification signals
- closing transition and next-step plan
