# Offer Positioning

Make the offer easy to understand and easy to want.

## Workflow

1. Define the buyer.
2. Define the core problem.
3. Define the desired outcome.
4. State why this offer is different.
5. Turn that into a short positioning statement.
6. End with the strongest messaging angle.

## Rules

- Avoid broad audience definitions.
- Avoid vague outcomes.
- Make the offer feel specific.
- Prefer one strong position over many weak claims.
- Keep the language concrete and usable in copy.

## Output expectation

Produce:
- audience
- problem
- outcome
- differentiation
- positioning statement
- strongest message angle
