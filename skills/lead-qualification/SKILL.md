# lead-qualification

Use this skill to quickly separate promising leads from weak or premature ones.

## Workflow

1. Identify the offer, audience, and sales context.
2. Extract what is known about the lead: need, timing, authority, budget, and urgency.
3. Check whether the lead clearly matches the offer.
4. Note the main risks, missing information, and weak signals.
5. Recommend the next sales action: pursue, nurture, clarify, or disqualify.

## Rules

- Keep qualification concrete and evidence-based.
- Distinguish curiosity from real buying intent.
- Do not invent budget, authority, or urgency if they are unknown.
- Call out uncertainty clearly instead of smoothing it over.

## Output expectation

Return:
- a short qualification summary
- lead quality rating
- main risks or unknowns
- recommended next step
